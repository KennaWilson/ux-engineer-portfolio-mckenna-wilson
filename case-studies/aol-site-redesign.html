Case 08

AOL Mail Redesign

A full UX and UI overhaul to improve clarity, modernize the visual language, and make key actions easier to complete.

Role
Lead Designer
Timeline
2 weeks
Team
Product Designer and Developer
Tools
Figma
View Case Work ↗

Problem

The legacy AOL Mail experience had an outdated visual system, weak hierarchy, and fragmented content paths that made common tasks harder than necessary.

Approach

I simplified the structure, created a new component-driven layout system, and introduced clearer CTA patterns that aligned to user intent across major page types.

  • Reorganized IA for primary user goals.
  • Designed reusable components and templates.
  • Improved accessibility, spacing, and typography scale.

Outcome

The redesign delivered a cleaner and more coherent experience while giving internal teams a scalable foundation for future content and features.
