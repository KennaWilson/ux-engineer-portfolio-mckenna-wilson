Domus Home Hub

An in-kitchen dashboard that helps busy families coordinate schedules, chores, and shared reminders with less friction.

Role
UX + Frontend + Backend
Timeline
2 weeks
Tools
Figma, React, TypeScript, Node.js, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
View the Domus Home Hub ↗ View Case Work ↗

Responsive for a kitchen dashboard app — would not recommend viewing on mobile.

Problem

Families were juggling multiple apps for tasks, calendars, and reminders. Critical home tasks were easy to miss, especially during school and work transitions. They were also overwhelmed by the amount of information they had to keep track of including chores, dinner plans, grocery lists, and family events.

Approach

I began with making simple low fidelity wireframes and prototypes to test the UI and interactions. I then moved on to making the high fidelity prototypes in Figma. The final UI prioritized glanceable status, quick add actions, and role-based permissions for parents and kids.

  • Interviewed households and analyzed pain-point patterns.
  • Built a modular component system for dashboard widgets.
  • Validated interactions through usability testing.

Outcome

The shipped experience reduced missed chores and schedule conflicts while giving families a shared source of truth in one place.
