Case 02

Agriculture and AI Integration

A decision-support experience that translates complex field data into clear recommendations farmers can act on quickly.

Role
Product Designer
Timeline
10 weeks
Team
Product Engineer
Tools
Figma, TypeScript
View the Agriculture AI Application ↗

Problem

Farmers had access to plenty of raw data, but no clear path from data to action. Existing tools were technical and time-consuming to interpret during busy workdays.

Approach

I ran collaborative sessions with farmers to map decisions across planting, irrigation, and harvest cycles. We designed an explainable recommendation engine UI that surfaced confidence, rationale, and next steps.

  • Conducted contextual interviews with farm operators.
  • Created task-first dashboards with visual alert states.
  • Designed AI recommendation cards with plain-language summaries.

Outcome

Users adopted the feature rapidly because recommendations felt trustworthy and practical, not black-box or overly technical.
