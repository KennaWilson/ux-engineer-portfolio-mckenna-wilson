Case 03

Wildhorse Creek Ranch Outfitters Website

A conversion-focused website redesign that clarified offerings, improved trust, and simplified the booking experience.

Role
UX + Frontend
Timeline
4 weeks
Team
Solo project
Tools
Figma, Next.js, HTML, CSS, React, and JavaScript
View Wildhorse Creek Ranch Outfitters Website ↗

Problem

The previous site had unclear navigation, inconsistent visual hierarchy, and a booking flow that caused drop-off before checkout.

Approach

I rebuilt the information architecture around user intent, created a clearer package comparison, and refined trust signals with stronger social proof and photography placement.

  • Audited booking journey and identified high-friction steps.
  • Redesigned key pages for readability and mobile use.
  • Implemented cleaner CTA strategy for inquiries and bookings.

Outcome

The new site improved discoverability and made booking actions much more direct, especially for mobile visitors planning trips on the go.

Impact
