Case Study 04

Kinship Website

End-to-end design and development of a new website for Kinship, a digital end of life planning platform.

Role
UX + Frontend + Design
Timeline
1 week
Tools
Figma & Design System

Problem

End of life planning is a complex and emotional process. Kinship needed a new website that would help users plan for the end of their life in a way that is easy to understand and use while still being compassionate and empathetic.

Approach

Created a design system and website for Kinship that is easy to understand and use while still being compassionate and empathetic.

  • Created a design system that is easy to understand and use while still being compassionate and empathetic.
  • Created a website that is easy to understand and use while still being compassionate and empathetic.
  • Tested the website with users to ensure it was easy to use and met their needs.

Outcome

A compassionate and empathetic website that helps users plan for the end of their life in a way that is easy to understand and use.

Highlights

100%
Website is compliant with accessibility standards
100%
Website is easy to understand and use
