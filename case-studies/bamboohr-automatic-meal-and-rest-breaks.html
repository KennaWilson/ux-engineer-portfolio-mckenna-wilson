Case Study 02

BambooHR Automatic Meal and Rest Breaks

Designed a new automated meal and rest break deduction feature for BambooHR, creating an intuitive experience for both HR managers and employees.

Role
UX + Frontend + Research + Design
Timeline
4 weeks
Tools
Figma, Claude Code, Maze, & Dovetail.

Problem

Companies were having a hard time managing meal and rest break deductions for their employees. Resulting in compliance issues and potential legal risks.

Approach

Conducted user research and interviews with customers around the globe to understand their pain points and needs. Worked with front-end developers to design the UI for the automated meal and rest break deduction feature in Figma and Claude Code.

  • Interviewed customers around the globe to understand their pain points and needs.
  • Worked with front-end developers to design the UI for the automated meal and rest break deduction feature in Figma and Claude Code.
  • Tested the feature with users to ensure it was easy to use and met their needs.

Outcome

The automated meal and rest break deduction feature helped companies manage meal and rest break deductions more efficiently and compliantly. Saving companies from potential legal risks and compliance issues.
